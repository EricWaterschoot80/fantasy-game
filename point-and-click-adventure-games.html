What are point-and-click adventure games?

Point-and-click adventure games are story-driven puzzle games where you explore hand-painted scenes, pick up objects, combine them and talk to characters to solve a mystery. There is no twitch reflex or fighting — just observation, logic and a good story. The genre was made famous by classics like Monkey Island, Day of the Tentacle and Broken Sword, and it lives on here in compact, modern 16-bit form.

Every adventure at RetroAdventureWorld is built by hand with pixel-art backgrounds, original characters and self-contained puzzles, so you can finish one in a single sitting.
