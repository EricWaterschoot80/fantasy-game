Whispers of Ravenholt — free 16-bit point-and-click mystery adventure

Play Whispers of Ravenholt, a free point-and-click adventure game in your browser — no download. In the kingdom of Eldoria the village fountain is running dry. Play as young Finn: question the mayor, trade a shiny coin with a clever raven for a hint, fix the silent water mill and brave the misty valley to uncover the secret of the lights. A pixel-art mystery for phone and desktop, in English and Dutch. Press Start to play.
